What We Do?

Maa Sharda Jan Kalyan Sewa Samiti is working in Prayagraj and Chitrakoot District.  We work on main 11 core pillars of social services that are Education, Skill Development, Old-Age Home, Environment, Rural Development, Agriculture, Gaushala, Health, Art and Culture, Women Empowerment, and Youth Development.
